About SWelK

SWelK is a mental health awarness and assessment tool. The anonymous Google assistent chatbox is designed to make one feel comfortable and calm while assessing their anxiety and stress levels.

SWelK provides:
  • A user friendly interface to communicate with
  • Stress and anxiety assessments
  • Viable options for addtional care
  • An anonymous platform to just talk

Download

SWelK comes as an extension to Google Assistant or Alexa.
